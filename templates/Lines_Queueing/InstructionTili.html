In the following rounds, you will interact under Take/leave: Under this rule, you can send an offer to the person in front of you. The requester needs to either accept or decline your offer. If accepted, both of you exchange positions and you will transfer your offer to the requester. You may also receive an offer from participants directly behind you and you will need to respond to that offer.

When you finish reading, please press next to take a quiz before you start the game. You can always review the instruction by clicking the "Review the Instruction" button at the end of the pages when you are at quiz pages and result pages. However, you cannot review the instruction when you are interacting with others.
