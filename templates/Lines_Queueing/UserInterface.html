Below shows the user interface of the experiment. The interface includes two panels:

{% block styles %} {% endblock %}

Upper panel shows the parameters and information of current round:
The first column shows the current round number.
The second column shows your own information: the value of service V, and your waiting cost C per “trading period”.
The third column shows the round information: Double\Swap\Take/Leave\Token, Communication\No Communication (You might only see some of them today)
The fourth column shows your current payoff.
The last column shows the remaining time in the current round (not the current trading period, notice the line will move forward during each round).

Lower panel shows the information of current waiting line and participants’ status:
The number of circles shows the number of players in your group. Your position is marked in black.
The line is divided into three parts which correspond to participants’ status: “waiting in the line”(left), “being served”(middle), and “service finished”(right).
The button on the bottom of each participants’ circle shows how you can interact with them. (not shown on the figure but you will see it in the game)

At the end of each round, you can review your game history from the previous round on your result page.
